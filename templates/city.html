Help

The normal weather reports are auto-generated.

There are four times of the day: morning, afternoon, evening, and night. Each report is generated based on the current weather conditions and forecast. Depending on the time of day, the report may emphasize different weather aspects.

The various styles will generate different report formats and adhere to specific guidelines of each style.

If you don't like a particular report style, you can switch to a different one using the tabs above. You can regenerate the report by clicking the "Generate report" button. Please be patient as it may take a few seconds to generate.

A report generated in the morning will no longer be available in the afternoon. Similarly, reports generated in the afternoon will not be available in the evening, and so on.
